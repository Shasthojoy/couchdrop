meet couchdrop

The secure, easy to use Cloud SCP file server.


Upload files securely to your cloud storage from Linux, Unix and OSX boxes without having to install any client software.
Files Delivered
Upload and Download files directly into Dropbox, Google Drive, Amazon S3 or WebDAV using SSH SCP. From server direct to your desktop.
Security Built In
Simple permissions, temporary users, files delivered directly to you.
Oneclick Setup
No server provisioning, port forwarding, firewalling.
Uploading Files
Uploading files is remarkably easy, just SCP them directly to couchdrop and within minutes they will appear in your Dropbox, Google Drive or AWS S3 accounts. You can then download these files in AWS, Dropbox or from the my.couchdrop web interface.


Instructions
scp file username@ssh.couchdrop.io:

Frequently Asked Questions
What is SCP?
SCP is a file transfer protocol coupled together with OpenSSH. It is more secure than FTP and is bundled by default with OSX, Unix and every Linux distribution.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secure_copy
Is it secure?
Yes! Security is paramount and a key motivator behind creating Couchdrop. We don't store your files on our servers and never intend on doing this. Files are stored in your cloud storage or on our Hosted Storage with Amazon S3.
Do we have to use Dropbox, Google Drive or Amazon AWS?
Couchdrop delivers files to your cloud storage provider rather than storing them on our servers. This means you can upload your files direct to your desktop from devices that do not support Dropbox or Amazon AWS storage and it's secure.

If you are hankering for some Hosted storage, then our Standard and Premium plans both come with Storage.
Is your product open source?
You put your trust in us, so we give something back to earn your trust. Trusting your files with a company always raises security concerns. That's why we have opensourced our product. Feel free to go through our implementation with a fine toothed comb.
